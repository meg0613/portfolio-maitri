Loaffa.

Brand Identity Typography Color Strategy Illustration Packaging

Loaffa is a modern bedding and sleepwear brand dedicated to creating a state of unhurried calm through the concept of the first touch. Built around premium 100% French linen, the identity transforms everyday rest into a sensory experience rooted in softness, breathability, and quiet luxury.

Process

The Loaffa identity was built around the sensory idea of “the first touch” translating the softness of French linen into a calm, visual language. A custom script logotype introduced warmth, while structured typography grounded the brand in modern clarity. The palette of custard cream, azure blue, and earth tones reflects sunlight, rest, and material honesty, creating a system that feels breathable, tactile, and intentional.

Result

The final identity transforms bedding into an emotional experience. By focusing on ritual and rest, Loaffa moves beyond product into lifestyle offering a quiet, premium presence that invites users to slow down. The cohesive brand system ensures every touchpoint feels effortless, warm, and deeply considered.

Melabody.

Brand Identity Visual Identity Packaging Design Web Design Social Media

Melabody is a modern, barrier-first body care brand engineered specifically for the physiological depth of melanin-rich skin. Moving beyond universal formulas that often fail to address specific structural needs, we combine dermatologist-backed science with rich, nourishing textures to eliminate ashiness and stop moisture loss at the source. The goal was to develop a full brand identity and packaging system that bridges the gap between scientific standards and deep-rooted cultural heritage.

Process

The design process for Melabody began by identifying the gap in the body care market for formulas that prioritize the physiological depth of melanin-rich skin. The strategy was to move beyond universal skincare by blending dermatologist-backed science with rich, ancestral textures to eliminate ashiness at the source. I developed a full brand identity and packaging system centered on a "Modern Heritage" aesthetic that feels both high-performance and soulful.

Result

The Melabody identity successfully established a premium space for specialized skin health that balances scientific authority with soulful heritage. The cohesive brand world and high performance packaging system have allowed for a seamless integration across digital and physical touchpoints. The visual narrative communicates deep rooted hydration and confidence, helping users feel truly seen.

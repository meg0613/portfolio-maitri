Uncle Dough.

Brand Identity Typography & Logo Menu Design Collateral Environmental Graphics

Uncle Dough is an artisanal neighborhood bakery aiming to bring the soul back to bread through long-fermented, handcrafted sourdough. Built around the persona of “the Uncle,” the brand embraces warmth, wit, and rustic authenticity—creating a bold yet inviting identity that stands apart from sterile, minimalist bakery trends.

Process

The process began with defining the “Uncle” persona as a blend of artisanal expertise and dry wit. This foundation guided a visual exploration of earthy, crust-inspired palettes and bold typography, capturing a grounded sense of tradition.

Each touchpoint was layered with a humanized narrative, ensuring the brand’s playful yet honest voice remained consistent across every application—from identity to storytelling. The system was then translated into physical deliverables, balancing texture, warmth, and clarity to evoke a true neighborhood bakery atmosphere.

Result

The final identity transforms Uncle Dough into a memorable neighborhood character rather than just a bakery. Moving away from cold minimalism, the brand now radiates warmth, personality, and authenticity—resonating with people who value craft and patience.

The cohesive system balances bold visuals with an approachable voice, creating an emotional connection that elevates everyday bread into a meaningful experience rooted in community.

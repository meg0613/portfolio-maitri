Astitva Bharat.

Brand Identity Spatial Design Typography Wayfinding Print Media

Astitva Bharat is a premium exhibition celebrating India's cultural identity through immersive regional worlds. The project blends heritage with modern luxury, creating a refined and narrative-driven experience system.

Process

The branding process explored the idea of identity through a balance of heritage and contemporary luxury. A modular grid system ensured consistency across applications, while typography combined elegance with clarity. Each visual decision contributed to a cohesive, premium experience.

Result

The final system transforms diverse cultural narratives into a unified brand experience. The identity enhances navigation, elevates presentation, and positions the exhibition as a refined global cultural destination.

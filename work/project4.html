Dhina Dhin Dha.

Brand Identity Editorial Design Magazine Layout Art Direction Photography Direction

Dhina Dhin Dha is a pop-culture magazine exploring the relationship between Bollywood films and the music that defines them. The project captures the energy, nostalgia, and cultural influence of Indian cinema through expressive editorial storytelling.

Process

The identity began with exploring the deep connection between Bollywood and music, using rhythm as a conceptual foundation. Editorial research guided the balance between expressive layouts and structured readability. Typography, imagery, and composition were crafted to evoke cinematic storytelling while maintaining clarity.

Result

The final magazine captures the vibrant spirit of Bollywood through a bold editorial system. It blends nostalgia with a contemporary voice, transforming cinema and music into a shared cultural narrative. The result is an expressive publication that reflects storytelling, rhythm, and identity across generations.
